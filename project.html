Welcome!

Chess is an abstract strategy game that involves no hidden information and no use of dice or cards. It is played on a chessboard with 64 squares arranged in an eight-by-eight grid. It is played using chess pieces called the King, Queen, Pawn, Bishop, Knight & the Castle. One of the team players can either play with the black set or the white set.
Benefits of playing chess include:

  • Increases memory by expanding brain cells which focuses on resilience building through a mind sport.
  • Chess involves imagination, thinking creatively, and the ability to find the best move under pressure.
  • Chess teaches you how to win and lose with grace.It is important to learn from your losses. You can come back stronger and wiser than before.
  • Chess helps you learn how to be calm under pressure.
